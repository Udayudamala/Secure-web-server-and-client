Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer and former captain of the Indian national cricket team. He was born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India.

Kohli is considered one of the best batsmen in the world, and has numerous records to his name. He made his international debut in 2008, and has since played in all three formats of the game - Test, ODI, and T20I.

He has been the recipient of several awards, including the Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy for ICC Cricketer of the Year in 2017 and 2018. He has also been named as one of Time magazine's 100 most influential people in the world in 2018.

Kohli is known for his aggressive and passionate style of play, and has led India to numerous victories on the cricket field. He is also a role model for many young cricketers in India and around the world.

Off the field, Kohli is actively involved in various social causes, and has also launched his own foundation - the Virat Kohli Foundation - which focuses on supporting underprivileged children.

Overall, Virat Kohli is a true champion of the game of cricket, and an inspiration to many.
